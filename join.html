Join the DAO

Ready to invest in on-chain ecosystems with OnChainDAO.Club? Follow these steps to become a member of our investment pool.

Steps to Join

  1. Join the OnChain Education Discord: Connect with our community on the official OnChain Education Discord. Or you can find the Discord link on the OnChain Education YouTube channel.
  2. Learn About Our Tier: The buy in to the fund is 100 USDC. If you have any questions, you can reach out on Discord or by contacting us.
  3. Apply During Openings: New members can join every two weeks. Check the Discord for the next application window.
  4. Contribute and Invest: Once approved, propose investment ideas and collaborate with our community of on-chain veterans.

Want to see how the Fund is performing? Check out the OnChain Education Youtube channel for updates about the fund's performance.
