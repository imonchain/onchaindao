The Approach

Longer-Term Investments

Risk Tolerance

The fund trades within the most volatile markets on the planet. Risk is what you sign up for in any speculative market, so keep that in mind before joining. If you are not used to on-chain volatility, you should not join the fund.

What we do

We take a more risk-averse approach to memecoin trading. We aim for smaller % gains than the status quo and we only make high conviction trades. We do not trade new pairs and we try not to buy coins below 1m marketcap. We thrive in the 10-50m range.
